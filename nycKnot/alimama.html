Story

Alimama Tea originated from a passion for creating unique bubble tea and desserts that celebrate Asian flavors with a modern twist. They are particularly known for their delightful cream puffs and mochi, which feature a variety of fillings and textures that elevate the dessert experience. Each treat is crafted with fresh ingredients, making them a perfect complement to the innovative drinks offered at Alimama Tea.
