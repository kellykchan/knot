Story

New Lake Pavilion has established itself as a premier destination for authentic Chinese cuisine in Queens, NY for its dim sum, seafood, and classic Chinese dishes, drawing in diners for a rich culinary experience. Known for seafood specialties and signature Chinese dishes, New Lake Pavilion is a top choice for authentic flavors and expertly prepared meals.
