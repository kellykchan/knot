Story

Zhong Zhong Noodles is a cozy eatery that specializes in authentic Chinese noodle dishes, drawing on traditional recipes to create flavorful meals. Since opening, Zhong Zhong Noodles has become a popular destination in the Bronx for those seeking satisfying and hearty noodle dishes.
