Story

Wonder Pig KBBQ was founded by food enthusiasts passionate about sharing authentic Korean barbecue culture with the community. The restaurant offers an interactive dining experience where guests grill their own high-quality meats at the table, creating a lively and social atmosphere. Since opening, it has become a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike, known for its commitment to fresh ingredients and customer satisfaction.
