Story

Bo Ky is a beloved spot known for its authentic Tewchow-style noodles and Vietnamese-Chinese fusion cuisine. Established by immigrants from Vietnam with roots in China, the restaurant brings together flavors that reflect both cultures, particularly through its noodle soups and spicy beef dishes. It has gained a loyal following for its affordable prices and nostalgic flavors, often reminding patrons of traditional Southeast Asian street food.
