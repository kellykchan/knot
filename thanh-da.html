Story

Thanh Da is a cherished spot for authentic Vietnamese cuisine, known especially for its fresh, flavorful pho and banh mi sandwiches. The restaurant prides itself on traditional recipes, with broths simmered for hours to bring out rich, aromatic flavors in every bowl of pho. Thanh Da’s banh mi, filled with savory meats, fresh herbs, and crisp vegetables on a crusty baguette, has become a go-to for those seeking a taste of Vietnam’s street food culture in a casual, welcoming setting.
